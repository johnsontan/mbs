Intensive hydrating facials are a great option for those with dry, dehydrated, or mature skin, or for anyone who wants to give their skin a boost of hydration and radiance. They can help to improve the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles, reduce inflammation, and leave the skin looking and feeling smoother and more youthful.

The specific products and techniques used during an intensive hydrating facial may vary depending on the individual's skin type and needs. However, common ingredients in hydrating facial treatments include hyaluronic acid, aloe vera, chamomile, and other nourishing botanical extracts.

  • Skin boost of hydration and radiance
  • Reduce inflammation
  • Reduce blemish and sensitive
  • Provide intense moisture to the skin
