Protein hair treatment involves the use of protein-based products to improve the health and appearance of the hair. Protein is an essential component of hair, and when hair is damaged due to factors such as heat styling, chemical treatments, or environmental stressors, the protein in the hair can become depleted. Protein hair treatments work by replenishing the protein in the hair, which can help to strengthen and repair the hair shaft.

  • Anti-irritant
  • Manageable hair
  • Hair hydration & elasticity
  • Replenish the protein in the hair
