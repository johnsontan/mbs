If you're looking for a way to rejuvenate your hair without exposing it to harmful chemicals, hair botox might be the solution. Unlike keratin and cysteine treatments, hair botox is a formaldehyde-free and non-chemical deep conditioning treatment that can help you fight frizz and improve the appearance of dull, damaged hair. During the treatment, a specialized product is applied to the surface of your hair, providing a protective coating that nourishes and strengthens each strand. With its restorative properties, hair botox can give you the lustrous, healthy-looking hair you've been dreaming of, without the risk of chemical damage.

It helps rebuild broken and thicken thin areas in your hair fibres to make them look and feel voluminous and soft.

  • Repair chemical damaged hair
  • Anti-frizz
  • Silky hair
  • Long lasting result
