Melon Beauty Salon history

Melon Beauty salon was founded in 1989, and it started at a small store in 682 Hougang Ave 8 and expanded to Hougang Central Block 811, #01-192. The outlet has temporarily moved to 1187 Upper Serangoon Rd, #01-52, while waiting for the Star of Kovan outlet to be built. As of the year 2021, we have shifted to Star of Kovan. Over the years, the company's primary goal has been still the same. It provides a one-stop service with a welcoming and comfortable environment for all our customer's pampering needs, a place where they are much-taken care of. The products used in Melon Beauty Salon are all-natural products.
